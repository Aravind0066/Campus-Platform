Platform Overview

Campus Intelligence is designed as a complete campus operations platform instead of a simple college website. It connects facility discovery, student collaboration, and official communication in one structured workflow.

Resources

Students can find labs, support offices, library spaces, and service points with building context, availability, timings, contact details, and equipment information.

Community

Students can post queries and temporary updates, reply in threaded discussions, and mark accepted answers so useful knowledge does not get lost in chat-style noise.

What Makes It Stand Out

The goal is not to overload the project with gimmicks. These are the features that make it feel complete, unique, and easy to explain in a presentation or viva.

Browser-bound sessions Sessions are tied to the authenticated browser, which adds a stronger security story than a basic login-only demo.
Email-powered notices Official notices can be imported from email and merged into the same noticeboard workflow instead of being treated as a disconnected inbox.
Short-lived update posts Community updates can expire, which fits real campus usage better than permanent clutter.
Accepted-answer discussion flow Student queries can now move from open question to resolved answer, making the platform more useful over time.
Building-linked resource intelligence Resources are not isolated records. They are tied to campus buildings, timings, support context, and operational details.
